This website is a NHL sports team calculator that allows you to enter any teams data from a season into the site to then give you insite on how they preformed.

The Team Record tab allows you to:

  1. Insert every game the team played in a season
  2. Each game entry consists of the opposing teams name and if your team won or lost the game
  3. At the bottom of all the entries it tells you how many games were played
  4. It also tells you if the team had a winning or lossing record and the corresponding game record

The Team Stats tab allows you to:

  1. Insert any players name, their position, how many points they scored and the predicted points scored for that player
  2. At the bottom it tells you the total number of points scored by all of the players
  3. It then tells you if the total points scored exceeded the predicted value or not for all of the players
  4. It also tells you who scored the most points out of all the players entered
